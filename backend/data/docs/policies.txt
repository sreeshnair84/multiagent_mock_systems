
# IT & HR Policies

## 1. Password Policy
All employees must rotate their passwords every 90 days. Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, containing a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. MFA is mandatory for all internal systems.

## 2. Remote Work Policy
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days a week with manager approval. Core hours are 10 AM to 3 PM local time.

## 3. Leave Policy
- Casual Leave: 12 days/year
- Sick Leave: 10 days/year
- Privilege Leave: 15 days/year (accrued)

## 4. IT Hardware Request Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)
1. Raise a request in ServiceNow.
2. Get approval from Line Manager (automatic if < $1000).
3. IT team will procure and provision within 5 business days.
4. Laptop options: MacBook Pro 14" (Devs), Dell XPS 13 (Sales).
